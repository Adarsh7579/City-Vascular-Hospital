Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about vascular health and our services.

What are Vascular Disorders?

Vascular disorders refer to conditions affecting the blood vessels, such as arteries and veins, leading to various health issues.

What is Vascular Imaging?

Vascular imaging involves using specialized techniques to visualize the blood vessels and diagnose vascular diseases and conditions.

What are Minimally Invasive Interventions?

Minimally invasive interventions are procedures that are performed through tiny incisions, reducing the need for traditional open surgery.

How Important are Vascular Screenings?

Vascular screenings are crucial for early detection and prevention of vascular diseases, helping to maintain optimal vascular health.

What is Vascular Rehabilitation?

Vascular rehabilitation involves a structured program to optimize recovery and improve the well-being of individuals with vascular conditions.
